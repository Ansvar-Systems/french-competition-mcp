# Coverage

## Authority

**Autorité de la concurrence (AdlC)**
Official French competition authority, established under Ordinance No. 2008-1161 of 13 November 2008.
Website: https://www.autoritedelaconcurrence.fr/

---

## Data Coverage

### Enforcement Decisions

- **Abuse of dominance** (abus de position dominante) — Article L.420-2 Code de commerce
- **Cartel enforcement** (ententes) — Article L.420-1 Code de commerce
- **Sector inquiries** (enquêtes sectorielles) — Article L.430-10 Code de commerce
- **Interim measures** (mesures conservatoires) — Article L.464-1 Code de commerce

### Merger Control

- Phase I clearance decisions (décisions d'autorisation en phase 1)
- Phase II decisions — clearance, conditional clearance, or prohibition
- Simplified procedure decisions

## Temporal Coverage

- **Decisions**: From approximately 2000 onwards (coverage may be incomplete for early years)
- **Mergers**: From approximately 2000 onwards

---

## Update Frequency

Database updates are periodic. Use `fr_comp_check_data_freshness` to check data age. Run `npm run ingest` to refresh from official AdlC sources.

---

## Not Covered

- Pending or unpublished decisions
- Confidential portions of decisions (non-confidential versions only)
- AdlC opinions (avis) — advisory opinions not binding
- Court appeals of AdlC decisions (Cour d'appel de Paris, Cour de cassation)
- EU-level enforcement by the European Commission (DG COMP)
- National court rulings applying competition law

---

## Legal Basis

AdlC jurisdiction under:
- Code de commerce, Articles L.420-1 to L.420-7 (anti-competitive practices)
- Code de commerce, Articles L.430-1 to L.430-10 (merger control)
- Regulation (EC) No 1/2003 (EU competition rules, for coordination cases with European Commission)
